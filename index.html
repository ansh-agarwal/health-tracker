The 4 rules

1. Protein first, every meal — dal/paneer/sprouts/channa/sattu before the roti/rice.
2. Half your plate = veg/salad. Eyeball it.
3. One heavy carb a day, not three (paranthas/rice once, ideally lunch).
4. Sattu on the commute, daily.

The plate template

Any meal, no maths: ½ veg/salad · ¼ protein · ¼ carb. Warm, fresh, eat to ¾ full.

The Ayurvedic edge

Lunch = biggest meal (agni peaks midday). Dinner = lightest, before sunset (khichdi/soup, no paranthas/curd at night). Warm water + lemon + honey on waking. 100-step walk after meals.

Targets & the slip rule

~110g protein, gentle deficit. 6 of 7 days = winning. Miss one? Nothing's ruined — next meal, back on. Monday weigh-in is the only number; daily just ask "4 rules — yes?"
